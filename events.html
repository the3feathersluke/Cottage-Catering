For weddings, we offer a range of catering options designed to suit your style and preferences. Whether you prefer classic dishes, modern twists, or something entirely unique, our team ensures every bite is a delight, creating a truly memorable dining experience for you and your guests on your special day.

Private Hire

For private parties, we offer a variety of catering options to suit any celebration. From stylish canapés and finger foods to full buffet spreads or plated dinners, we’ll craft a menu that perfectly fits your theme and guest preferences. Whether you're hosting a casual get-together or an upscale soirée, our flexible catering options ensure your event is deliciously memorable, with food that delights and service that impresses.

Dry Hire

For those looking to manage their own event setup, we offer dry hire of our catering unit, providing you with the flexibility to create your perfect event. Our fully equipped unit comes with everything you need to serve your guests, from preparation space to professional-grade equipment. Whether you’re hosting a large event or a smaller gathering, our dry hire option allows you to bring your vision to life while we provide the tools and support to make it a success.

V-Festival

Saturday 22nd August - Weston Park

V Festival is Englands first festival to be held simultaneously at two different sites, Hylands Park in Chelmsford and Weston Park in South Staffordshire. V standing for Virgin Group are the major sponsors of the festival which never fails to deliver a stellar lineup year after year. Although predominantly a rock festival, V is known to play host to some of the biggest pop, rap and hiphop stars from around the globe, artists such as Kayne West, N.E.R.D and Girls Aloud. Sold out year after year, this festival is not one to be missed!
